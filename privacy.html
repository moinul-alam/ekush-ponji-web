01

Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Create

When you use Ekush Ponji, you may create the following content which is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Personal events and reminders you add to the calendar
  • Quotes you save from the daily quotes feature
  • Words you save from the daily words feature
  • App preferences and settings (language, theme, notification preferences)

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

We do not directly collect any personal information. However, our third-party advertising partner, Google AdMob, may automatically collect certain information to serve advertisements. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • IP address (used to determine approximate country-level location)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • App usage data relevant to ad serving

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

1.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Photos, media, or files from your device
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or biometric data
02

How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information directly, our use of data is limited to:

  • Delivering app functionality — your locally stored events, reminders, saved quotes, and saved words are used solely to provide the features you requested
  • Serving advertisements — Google AdMob uses device and usage data to serve relevant ads within the App
  • Content delivery — the App fetches daily quotes and words from our static content repository hosted on GitHub. No personal data is transmitted during this process
03

Advertising

Ekush Ponji uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your device settings and applicable regional laws.

3.1 Personalized Ads

If personalized ads are enabled, AdMob uses your Advertising ID and usage patterns to show ads relevant to your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

3.2 Non-Personalized Ads

If you opt out of personalized ads, AdMob will serve contextual ads that are not based on your personal interests. Some data may still be collected by AdMob for fraud prevention and frequency capping purposes.

3.3 Children & Ads

Ekush Ponji is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly serve targeted advertisements to children.

For more information about how Google uses data in advertising, visit: Google Advertising Policies.

04

Data Storage & Security

4.1 Local Storage

All user-generated data (events, reminders, saved quotes, saved words, and settings) is stored locally on your device using Android's secure local storage. This data is not backed up to any external server by us.

4.2 Content Repository

Daily quotes and words are fetched from a static content repository hosted on GitHub. These requests do not include any personal information — only a standard HTTP request is made to retrieve the content.

4.3 Data Retention

Your locally stored data is retained on your device until you:

  • Delete it manually within the App
  • Use the "Clear All Data" option in App Settings
  • Uninstall the App

We do not retain any copy of your data on our servers as we do not receive it in the first place.

4.4 Security

We rely on Android's built-in security model to protect locally stored data. We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the App from unauthorized access. However, no method of storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

05

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data at all times.

5.1 Deleting Your Data

You can delete all locally stored data directly within the App:

  • Open Ekush Ponji
  • Go to Settings
  • Tap Clear All Data
  • Confirm the action

This will permanently delete all your events, reminders, saved quotes, saved words, and preferences from your device.

5.2 Uninstalling the App

Uninstalling Ekush Ponji from your device will remove all locally stored app data.

5.3 Advertising Data

To reset or delete your Advertising ID used by Google AdMob, go to your device's Settings → Google → Ads and select "Delete advertising ID".

5.4 Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your data, or if you believe any data has been collected incorrectly, please contact us at:

ekushlabs@gmail.com

07

App Permissions

Ekush Ponji requests the following Android permissions:

Internet Access

Required to fetch daily quotes and words from our content repository, and to load advertisements via Google AdMob.

Notifications

Required to deliver reminders you set within the App. Notifications are only sent based on reminders you explicitly create. You can disable this in your device settings at any time.

Receive Boot Completed

Required to reschedule your reminders after the device restarts, ensuring you never miss a reminder you have set.

No other permissions are requested. We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or storage beyond what is needed for local app data.
